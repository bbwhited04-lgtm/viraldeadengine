Tracking that doesn’t annoy users

Measurable, not creepy

Every CTA link is tagged with UTMs and can log a click event to your backend. You get attribution without popups, without clutter.

Tip: Set window.VD_TRACKING.endpoint to your deployed API and you’re live.
