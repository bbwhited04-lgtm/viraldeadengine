A funnel that feels clean, not salesy

4 min read • Practical. Calm. Repeatable.
#funnel #ux #conversion

Your funnels should feel like a quiet hallway, not a maze. The goal is to reduce hesitation and make the next step obvious.

What to put on every page

  • Promise: what changes for them
  • Proof: what they get + how it works
  • Path: one clear button

Cross-links (done right)

You asked for small links to your other properties. Keep them subtle: footer only. That protects focus while still strengthening your network.

Rule: Keep cross-links small, consistent, and non-distracting.
